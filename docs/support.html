Frequently Asked Questions

Why are there ads in the game?

Our games are completely free to download and play. Advertisements help us keep the games free while continuing to develop new content and improvements.

Can I remove the ads?

Currently, there is no option to remove ads. We are exploring ad-free options for future updates.

The game is crashing or not loading. What should I do?

Try the following steps: (1) Close the app completely and reopen it. (2) Restart your device. (3) Make sure you have the latest version installed from the App Store. (4) If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS version.

How do I reset my game progress?

You can reset your game progress by deleting and reinstalling the app. Please note that all scores and progress will be permanently lost.

What is the "Allow Tracking" popup?

On iOS 14.5 and later, Apple requires apps to ask your permission before using your device's advertising identifier. This is used solely for showing more relevant ads. You can decline without affecting gameplay.

Do you collect my personal data?

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. For full details, please see our Privacy Policy.
